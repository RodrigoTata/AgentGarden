# Grill Me — Black_Rg

## Objective
Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide two alternatives, recommend one telling the why. Also leave room for open answers and questions related to your questions.

## Rules
1. Ask the questions one at a time.
2. If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead of asking.
3. Provide two alternatives with recommendations (explaining the why).
4. Leave room for open answers and follow-up questions.
5. Do not skip branches — resolve every decision point before moving forward.
